## Short Description (132 chars max)
Organize your NotebookLM notebooks with folders, drag & drop, search, and a slide prompt generator. Clean, fast, zero dependencies.

## Detailed Description

Tired of scrolling through a flat list of notebooks in NotebookLM? FolderLM adds the folder system that NotebookLM is missing.

🗂 ORGANIZE WITH FOLDERS
Create nested folders to group your notebooks by project, topic, or however you like. Drag and drop notebooks between folders with ease.

🔍 SEARCH & FILTER
Instantly find any notebook with the built-in search bar. Filter your workspace by folder to focus on what matters.

⭐ FAVORITES
Pin your most-used notebooks for one-click access.

🎨 THEMES
FolderLM automatically matches your system's light or dark mode, blending seamlessly into the NotebookLM interface.

🎬 SLIDE PROMPT GENERATOR
Generate AI presentation prompts with custom color palettes, fonts, and tones — a unique tool built for NotebookLM power users.

💾 EXPORT & IMPORT
Back up your workspace configuration anytime. Move your setup to a new device in seconds.

PRIVACY FIRST
All data stays on your device. FolderLM uses zero external servers, zero analytics, zero tracking. Your notebooks are yours.

LIGHTWEIGHT
Zero dependencies. Pure JavaScript. No build tools. Fast and minimal by design.
